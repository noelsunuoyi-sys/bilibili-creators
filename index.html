Your first week

Want personalized support from day one?

You won't figure out a new platform alone. Your dedicated manager works with you on the moves that matter early — what to post, when, and how to grow fast. Here's a taste of the playbook they'll tailor to your channel.

What your manager helps you nail High impact

  • A self-intro that converts. Under 2 minutes, your skill shown in the first 15 seconds, pinned to your profile.
  • A hook in the first 3 seconds. Open on your strongest moment — we'll help you find it.
  • A posting rhythm that the platform rewards. The right days, times and cadence for your niche.
  • Early engagement that compounds. How to turn your first commenters into a founding community.

And the traps they help you skip Save your energy

  • Chasing perfection. A good video today beats a perfect one next week.
  • Spreading too thin. One clear lane for your first videos so the platform reads your signal.
  • Over-editing. Simple cuts are standard here — no elaborate transitions needed.
  • Guesswork. We share what's already working for other international creators.
